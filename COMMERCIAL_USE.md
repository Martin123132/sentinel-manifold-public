# Commercial Use

Sentinel Manifold is source-available so people can inspect it, test it, learn
from it, and use it for personal, community, research, nonprofit, educational,
evaluation, and small-business work.

The project is not a free-for-all commercial grant.

## Allowed Without Asking

- Personal projects, study, research, hobby work, and private experiments.
- Noncommercial use by individuals and community groups.
- Educational, nonprofit, public research, public safety, public health, and
  similar public-interest use covered by the PolyForm Noncommercial License.
- Small-business use covered by the PolyForm Small Business License.
- Non-production evaluation, benchmarking, compatibility testing, security
  testing, and internal proof-of-concept work.

## Ask For A Commercial License

You need a written commercial license before using Sentinel Manifold in or as
part of:

- a commercial AI platform, LLM provider, model gateway, or agent platform;
- a search engine, advertising product, analytics product, or ranking system;
- a hosted service, API, customer-facing SaaS product, or paid developer tool;
- a product feature that helps generate revenue for a company outside the small
  business grant;
- a commercial derivative, integration, resale package, or managed service.

Commercial licences can include financial terms, attribution, deployment limits,
support, private features, or community benefit commitments.

The intended bargain is straightforward: ordinary people and small builders can
use the work without friction; large commercial platforms should ask, pay fairly,
and leave room for the project to benefit the communities it came from.
